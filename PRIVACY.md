# Privacy Policy for KOMA Manga Reader

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-03

## 1. Introduction
KOMA Manga Reader ("KOMA", "we", "us", or "our") is a free, open-source comic reader for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and what happens to your information when you use the KOMA application.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**
KOMA is built as a fully local application. All of your data, including your reading history, library, downloaded chapters, and settings, are stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that collect telemetry, usage analytics, or personal information.

## 3. Third-Party Extensions and Sources
KOMA acts as a specialized web browser. It allows you to browse and read content from various third-party websites through installable extensions.
- When you use an extension to browse or read content, your device connects directly to that third-party website.
- Your IP address and browsing requests are exposed to those third-party websites just as they would be if you visited them using a standard web browser like Chrome or Firefox.
- We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sources.

## 4. Tracking Services
KOMA offers optional integration with third-party tracking services (such as MyAnimeList, AniList, Kitsu, MangaUpdates, etc.).
- If you choose to log into these services through KOMA, the app will communicate directly with their respective APIs to update your reading progress.
- We do not intercept or store your login credentials on any external servers. Your credentials are saved locally on your device.

## 5. Device Permissions
To function properly, KOMA may request certain permissions on your Android device:
- **Storage:** To save downloaded chapters and create local backups of your library.
- **Network:** To connect to third-party sources for fetching comic content and updates.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
Since KOMA is an open-source project, any changes to how the application handles data will be fully transparent and visible in our public source code repository. We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our app.

## 7. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the KOMA Manga Reader, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
